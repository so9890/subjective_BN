\subsection{Model homo skill, exo growth, no knowledge spills, equal capital shares}

With knowledge spillovers, net emissions reduce, bcs the fossil sector grows less and the green sector grows more. Demand switches to green energy. Indeed, in BAu there is a higher green to fossil production. the energy share grows less because the non-energy sector grows faster. Green price relative to fossil price is falling over time since the green sector catches up. 

But also the effect of tauf on green to fossil production is muted in future periods, bcs fossil grows as green sector grows. \textbf{\tr{\ar higher fossil taxes to meet emission limit? and motive to use labor taxes to reduce research overall?}}
With endogenous growth this difference should be intensified as the direction of research reacts. Even bigger surge in fossil production in later periods when tauf is constant.
