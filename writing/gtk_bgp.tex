\section{Got to know: Balanced growth}

\begin{itemize}
	\item the assumption of balanced growth follows from evidence that over the long run certain ratios are constant
	\cite{Herrendorf2014GrowthTransformation}
	\item the literature on structural transformation seeks to reconcile balanced growth of some ratios while mainly across sector ratios such as labor inputs are allowed to vary
	(generalized balanced growth path )
	\item the assumption simplifies the calculation of infinite horizon optimization problems
	\item but it comes at the cost of assuming certain parameter values and forcing additional assumptions on the model structure (eg some ratios are constant)
\end{itemize}
